Ordering

To place an order, browse restaurants, select items, and proceed to checkout. You can pay online or choose cash on delivery.

You can modify your order within 5 minutes of placing it. After that, please contact customer support for assistance.

Delivery

Delivery hours vary by restaurant. Most restaurants deliver from 10 AM to 10 PM daily.

You can track your order in real-time from the Order History section of the app.
